Privacy policy

SOCOMART collects only the information needed to process your orders: your name, phone number, email, and delivery address. This information is used solely to fulfil orders, provide customer support, and — with your consent — send offers via our newsletter.

Order data is stored securely and, where a backend is connected, written to a private Google Sheet accessible only to the SOCOMART team. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your browser's local storage is used to remember your cart, wishlist, and preferences on this device — this data never leaves your browser unless you complete a checkout.

For any privacy questions, reach us via the contact page.
